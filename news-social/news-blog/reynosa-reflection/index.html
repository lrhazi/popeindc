Concertmaster for the Papal Mass, Enrique Reynosa, Thanks CUA Faculty

By Patricia Diaz Suzarte
Posted January 28, 2016
In Reflections
  0

I had the honor to serve as the concertmaster for the orchestra that performed for the papal Mass. I have to say that it was an experience I never thought I would have.

We worked very hard under the masterful guidance of Maestro Simeone Tartaglione; we wanted to be deserving of such an honor. To play for the Pope was a once-in-a-lifetime experience that I would possibly never have if I were not currently enrolled at Catholic University. I would like to express my gratitude to all the faculty of the Benjamin T. Rome School of Music for their never-ending dedication to us the students. When talent meets inspiration the results are extraordinary.

Enrique Reynosa is a doctoral student in musical arts, violin performance at The Catholic University of America.
