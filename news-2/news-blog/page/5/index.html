This website is brought to you by The Catholic University of America. The mission for this site is to provide information about the visit of Pope Francis to the University, the papal Mass held on September 23, 2015, and the lasting impact of the Pope's visit on the University. The students, faculty, and staff of the University were honored to welcome the Holy Father to our campus. For additional information regarding this website, please contact us.
